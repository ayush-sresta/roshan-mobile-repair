Our Journey

"From a small shop in London to a trusted mobile & laptop repair brand."

Roshan Mobile Repair (RMR) started with a single goal — to offer fast, affordable, and trustworthy mobile repair services in the heart of London. What began as a humble local shop has grown into a brand known for quality service, genuine parts, and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we’ve successfully repaired thousands of devices — from iPhones and Samsung phones to laptops and tablets. We’ve expanded our offerings to include gadget accessories like chargers, covers, earbuds, and more.

Our commitment remains the same: fast service, transparent pricing, and happy customers. RMR isn’t just a shop — it’s a reliable partner for all your tech needs.

Our Mission

At Roshan Mobile Repair, our mission is to deliver fast, honest, and affordable repair services while maintaining the highest standards of customer care. We aim to bring devices back to life and smiles back to our customers’ faces—every single day.

Our Vision

We envision becoming the UK’s most trusted name in gadget repair and accessories—empowering customers with quality, speed, and value, both in-store and online. Our goal is to expand with multiple branches, while never compromising our personal touch.

Meet Our Founder – Roshan Kumar Acharya

Roshan Kumar Acharya is the founder and lead technician at Roshan Mobil(RMR). Originally from Dharan, Nepal and now based in London, UK, Roshan brings years of hands-on experience in mobile and electronics repair. He studied iPhone Repairing at FIXST Maintenance College and has worked with top service centers like Advance Solution Center, Refixtronic, and Smart Tech Coffee Eltham. His background also includes technical roles as an Engineering Technician and Electrician. With a deep passion for mobile technology and a commitment to quality service, Roshan ensures every repair is handled with care, precision, and trust.
